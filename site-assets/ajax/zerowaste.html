Project

Fraunhofer ZeroWaste
(2022, Industry Event)
by FRIDIE for Fraunhofer CCPE

Description

Industry Event Application
Showcasing a smart recycling process and the resulting elements including their distribution.
Input is touch and additional cylinders with RFID-tags representing different waste products for recycling can be placed and tracked to switch between modes. Integrated with a Phidget microcontroller.

Tech

Unity, C#, RFID, Phidget22

Roles

Lead Developer
